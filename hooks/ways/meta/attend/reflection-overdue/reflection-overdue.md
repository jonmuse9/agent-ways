---
description: Prompt reflection when context is high and no recent ledger capture
trigger:
  type: attend
  signals:
    - reflection-overdue
scope: agent
refire: 0.15
---

Context usage is elevated and no reflection has been captured recently.
This is the window closing — take a moment to capture what matters:

1. **What decisions were made** and why — the reasoning behind choices
2. **What didn't work** and what you learned from it
3. **Current state** — what's in progress, what's blocked, what's next
4. **Save to memory** anything the next session needs to know

A brief, honest reflection is more valuable than a comprehensive one.
Three sentences about what surprised you is worth more than a page of
status reporting.
